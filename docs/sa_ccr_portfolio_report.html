Calculation Methodology

Core SA-CCR Formula

EAD = alpha * (RC + PFE) alpha = 1.4 PFE = multiplier * AddOn_aggregate Unmargined RC = max(V - C, 0) Margined RC = max(V - C, TH + MTA - NICA, 0)

Data Separation

Public SDR fields provide product economics such as asset class, notional, currency, dates, cleared flag, product identifiers, and underlier information.

Synthetic fields are added for internal bank concepts not publicly disclosed: counterparty, legal netting set, CSA terms, collateral, MtM, direction, and risk weight.

1. Data validationCheck required fields, parse dates, and remove expired trades.
2. Trade enrichmentAssign asset class, product type, hedging set, supervisory factor, option volatility, and maturity bucket.
3. Adjusted notionalUse supervisory duration for interest rate and credit trades; use converted notional or base notional for other products.
4. Delta and maturity factorApply option delta where option fields exist; use MPOR for margined trades and remaining maturity for unmargined trades.
5. Add-on aggregationAggregate by IR maturity bucket, FX pair, credit/equity entity, and commodity group/type.
6. Exposure and capitalCalculate multiplier, PFE, RC, EAD, illustrative RWA, and margined netting-set EAD cap.

Assumptions, Controls, and Limitations

Implemented Controls

  • Data-quality check file is generated for every run.
  • Trade-level enrichment is exported for review and audit tracing.
  • Netting-set exposure output includes RC formula, EAD before cap, unmargined EAD cap, and cap-applied flag.
  • Unit tests cover RC floor, margined RC formula, PFE multiplier floor, basis factor adjustment, and pipeline execution.

Remaining Production Gaps

  • Legal netting enforceability and CSA eligibility are not modeled.
  • Collateral haircuts and full initial/variation margin operations are simplified.
  • Risk weights are illustrative rather than generated from a regulatory credit risk engine.
  • Market data conversion uses a static table for reproducibility.
Interview positioning: this is best described as a Basel-aligned educational SA-CCR capital engine using real public trade economics and synthetic internal-bank fields. It demonstrates product classification, trade enrichment, exposure aggregation, capital logic, controls, and limitations.
